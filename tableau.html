Data Visualization using Tableau

These projects highlight my proficiency in Tableau, emphasizing my ability to create insightful and interactive visualizations from complex data sets.

HR Attrition Data Report

The aim of this dashboard project is to identify the factors contributing to employee churn and provide actionable insights to reduce turnover, improve retention, and enhance overall organizational performance. The dashboard highlights job satisfaction ratings across various roles and examines attrition rates based on education, department, gender, and age group. These insights can help employers develop strategies to retain employees, improve workplace quality, and foster a more engaging work environment.

Amazon Prime Video Data Analysis

This dashboard provides insights into top-rated categories and identifies the countries that produce the most shows and movies on Prime Video. It offers detailed descriptions of movies and TV shows, including cast, release year, duration, and genre. Additionally, the dashboard highlights the most popular genres and tracks the release trends over the years. This information can assist the company in making informed decisions about which shows to produce in the future to maximize profitability.

Electric Vehicle Data Analysis

The aim of this project was to create a dashboard that tracks the total number of electric vehicles (EVs) on the road since 2011, their average electric range, and the variety of EV types. The dashboard presents a comparison of vehicle production trends over the years and examines EV usage across different U.S. states. It also features the top 10 EVs by make and includes a detailed table showcasing various models along with their CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility. This dashboard not only aids companies in planning for future production but also highlights the growing public interest in transitioning to electric vehicles.
