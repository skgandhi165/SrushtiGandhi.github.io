E-Commerce Sales Analysis

This project highlights my Excel skills, particularly in using pivot tables and conditional formatting, to create a comprehensive dashboard. The dashboard enables detailed analysis of sales and profit metrics, showcasing total sales, orders, profit, quantity sold, and profit margins, as well as year-on-year growth. It also reveals category-wise profit and sales shares and identifies the U.S. state with the highest sales volume. Additionally, the dashboard displays the best-selling items and allows for filtering based on year, region, or segment.
